Lost in Pandation
A 72h Ludum Dare game by Sebastien Benard
------------------------------------------

GAMEPAD CONTROLS (XBOX CONTROLLER):
	LEFT STICK - walk around
	A - Jump
	X - Use/Pick up
	B - Drop item
	SELECT - Restart

KEYBOARD CONTROLS
	LEFT/RIGHT - walk around
	UP - jump
	DOWN - Drop item
	SPACE - Use/Pick up
	R - Restart
	M - Toggle music
